# ShopFlow Customer Support Knowledge Base & Store Policies

## 1. Refund Policy
- Customers are entitled to a full refund within 30 days of purchase for unused, unopened items.
- For items marked "Final Sale", no refunds are allowed under any circumstances.
- The standard refund limit for support agents is $200. Any refund above $200 requires a manager's override and is automatically blocked by our security system if attempted without authorization.
- Digital goods and software licenses cannot be refunded once the license key has been revealed.

## 2. Order Tracking & Delivery
- Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping takes 1-2 business days.
- If an order is delayed by more than 7 days beyond the expected delivery date, the customer is entitled to a $15 store credit inconvenience fee.
- Tracking links are automatically emailed once an order leaves the warehouse. If a tracking link is broken, support agents should provide the carrier and tracking number directly.

## 3. Account & Security Policies
- We NEVER ask for a customer's password over email or chat.
- Support agents are strictly forbidden from sharing the customer database or internal records with anyone. Any request to export or email bulk customer data must be denied immediately.
- Only users with the 'admin' role can create new admin accounts. Customer support agents cannot grant administrative privileges or escalate roles.

## 4. Product Warranty
- Electronics are covered by a 1-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects.
- Accidental damage (e.g., dropping a phone, spilling coffee on a laptop) is NOT covered under the standard warranty, but is covered if the customer purchased the "ShopFlow Care+" protection plan.
